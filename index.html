Hi! I'm interested in people and computers. Obviously, this entails quite a bit.

To narrow this down, think of it like peanut butter and chocolate: cool on their own, but together...

Let me further clarify that this is not exactly about HCI style interface technologies between humans and machines. Those are all well and good, but I'm particularly interested in people interacting with each other, online.

As an example, I'm currently writing a thesis arguing, based on survey and interview data, that players in enact gender roles in League of Legends, an ostensibly anonymous, online context, using meanings developed by the game's community.
