To narrow it down further, let me clarify that this is not exactly HCI. My sense of that is it's more about emerging interface technologies between humans and machines. Which is all well and good, but I'm particularly interested in people interacting with each other, online.

As a clarifying example, I'm currently writing a thesis arguing, based on survey and interview data, that players in enact gender roles in League of Legends, an ostensibly anonymous, online context, using meanings developed by the game's community.
